We collect the following information when you interact with our website or enroll in our programs:

3 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate and improve our services, and to serve you relevant advertising.

Cookie / Technology Provider Purpose
_ga, _gid, _gat Google Analytics Website traffic analysis, user behavior
_gcl_au, GCLID Google Ads Conversion tracking, ad performance measurement

4 Google Ads & Remarketing Disclosure

We use Google Ads (including Demand Gen and Search campaigns) to promote our bootcamp. This involves:

  • Using Google Conversion Tracking to measure when a user who clicked our ad completes an enrollment or checkout action
  • Using Google Remarketing (via Google tag / gtag.js) to show our ads to past visitors across the Google Display Network and YouTube
  • Collecting and transmitting your Google Click ID (GCLID) to our backend for server-side conversion attribution
  • Using Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand user behavior on our website

🔍 Your Advertising Choices with Google

Google uses cookies and device identifiers to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting google.com/settings/ads.

You can also opt out via the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

To learn more about how Google uses data, visit: How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

5 Meta Pixel & Conversions API

We use the Meta Pixel (browser-side) and Meta Conversions API (CAPI) (server-side) to measure the effectiveness of our Facebook and Instagram advertising campaigns.

  • This allows Meta to attribute conversions (enrollments) to the correct ad campaigns
  • We may share hashed versions of your email or phone number with Meta via CAPI for matching purposes
  • All data is hashed (SHA-256) before transmission and is not shared in plain text

To opt out of Meta's use of your data for ad targeting, visit your Facebook Ad Preferences.
